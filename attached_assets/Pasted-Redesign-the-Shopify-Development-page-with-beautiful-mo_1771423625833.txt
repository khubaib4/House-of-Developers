Q1: "How much does Shopify development cost?"

Q2: "Should I use Shopify or WooCommerce?"
A: "Shopify is better for pure e-commerce—easier to manage, 
better hosting, built-in payments. WooCommerce is better if 
you need extensive customization or complex content alongside 
products. We can advise which fits your business best."

Q3: "What's the difference between Shopify and Shopify Plus?"
A: "Shopify Plus ($2,000/month) adds: custom checkout, 
unlimited API calls, Shopify Scripts, B2B/wholesale features, 
automation (Flow), and priority support. Recommended for 
stores doing $1M+ annual revenue."

Q4: "Can you migrate my store to Shopify?"
A: "Yes. We migrate from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, 
custom platforms, or old Shopify stores. Products, customers, 
orders, and SEO transferred with zero downtime. Typical 
migration: 2-4 weeks."

Q5: "How long does Shopify development take?"
A: "Basic Shopify store: 4-6 weeks. Custom theme: 8-12 weeks. 
Shopify Plus with complex features: 12-20 weeks. Timeline 
depends on scope, product count, and integrations."

Q6: "Do you build custom Shopify apps?"
A: "Yes. We build private apps for your specific store and 
public apps for the Shopify App Store. Custom apps for unique 
functionality not available in existing apps."

Q7: "Can Shopify handle high traffic and sales?"
A: "Absolutely. Shopify is designed for scale. The platform 
handles 10,000+ orders per minute during peak times. We've 
built stores doing millions in revenue with no performance 
issues. 99.99% uptime guaranteed by Shopify."

Q8: "What payment methods can I accept on Shopify?"
A: "Shopify Payments (Stripe), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, 
Shop Pay, Klarna, Afterpay, and 100+ other payment gateways. 
We set up all payment methods and test thoroughly."

Q9: "Can I sell internationally with Shopify?"
A: "Yes. Shopify supports multi-currency (130+ currencies), 
multi-language stores, international shipping, customs forms, 
and duties calculation. We set up global selling properly."

Q10: "Is Shopify good for subscriptions?"
A: "Yes. Apps like ReCharge and Bold Subscriptions power 
subscription boxes, memberships, and recurring products. 
We've built 10+ subscription stores on Shopify."

Q11: "What Shopify apps do you recommend?"
A: "Essentials: Klaviyo (email), Yotpo or Judge.me (reviews), 
Gorgias (support), ReCharge (subscriptions), Privy (popups). 
Depends on your business model—we advise on the right apps."

Q12: "Do you offer Shopify maintenance after launch?"
A: "Yes. Every project includes 60 days post-launch support. 
After that, maintenance packages from £200/month covering 
app updates, theme changes, bug fixes, and performance 
monitoring."
